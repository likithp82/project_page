Video At A Glance: Merging Space and Time from a Video in Single Image

Video At a Glance transforms videos into a single image that captures their full spatial and temporal essence.

Abstract

We propose a transformative framework aimed at condensing a video sequence into a single, comprehensive frame that captures the core narrative of the entire video.

Our framework comprises a series of interconnected modules, including optical flow estimation, segmentation, background mon- tage construction, inpainting, and foreground handling. Each module is intricately designed to ensure that both spatial and temporal details are preserved, ultimately synthesizing what we term a Space-Time Photo. This approach not only maintains the integrity of the original video content but also significantly enhances the viewer's experience by presenting a complete and unified narrative in a singular image.

Downstream Tasks

Removing Foreground Object from the Video Using the Background Representation

We leverage the Background Montage from the Space-Time Image construction to effectively remove foreground objects. By mapping and reversing foreground pixels onto the Montage, we replace them in the original frames with inferred background colors from the Montage.

Video Summarization using Space Time Image

By integrating image-to-text language models, we can extend the visual summary of Space Time Image to generate textual descriptions, enabling efficient video captioning and making the summarization process more accessible.

Creation of Videos with Wider Field of View and Effects using Background Montage and Extracted Foreground Objects

We utilize the background montage, along with extracted foreground objects and their mappings, to generate dynamic video sequences. By moving an HxW window over the montage, we create frames that showcase different perspectives and movements. This technique enables freezing moments in time, zooming into objects of interest, navigating across the montage, and adjusting the field of view by varying the window size.

Foreground Object Completion Using Foreground Object Completion Module

Our Foreground Object Completion module, a modified Stable Diffusion Inpainting model inspired by Tune-A-Video and RealFill Inpainting Network, addresses scenarios where foreground objects move out of view in video frames. By temporally inpainting these missing regions, it ensures seamless completion of objects across frames. This enhancement significantly improves the visual consistency and integrity of the Space-Time Image, making it more comprehensive and visually appealing.

Video Clustering Using Space Time Image

We cluster videos by generating a Space-Time Image for each video, capturing its key narrative in a single snapshot. Using CLIP, we create image embeddings from these snapshots and apply a clustering algorithm to group similar videos. This efficient method improves video organization, recommendation, and retrieval, simplifying the management and analysis of large video datasets.

Conclusion

"Video at a Glance" is an innovative framework designed to distill comprehensive video sequences into a single, informative image—the Space-Time Photo. The Space-Time Photo serves as an exceptional visualization tool, summarizing the content seen in a video within a single snapshot. This innovative representation allows users to grasp the entire narrative and significant events of the video at a glance, facilitating quicker and more efficient video content review. It provides a condensed yet comprehensive visual summary, making it a valuable asset for various applications, from video editing and archiving to content analysis and surveillance.
